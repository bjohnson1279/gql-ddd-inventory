## 2026-06-03 - Fix insecure UUID generator
**Vulnerability:** The application was using an insecure method `Math.random().toString(36).substring(2, 15)` to generate unique identifiers (UUIDs) across the codebase.
**Learning:** `Math.random()` is not a cryptographically secure pseudorandom number generator (CSPRNG), making the generated identifiers predictable and prone to collisions, which could lead to data integrity and security issues.
**Prevention:** Always use a standard, cryptographically secure UUID generator like `crypto.randomUUID()` to generate unique identifiers.
